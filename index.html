About Me

I am a UX and UI Designer from Mexico.

I started working in design while simultaneously studying. I had to use most of the tools and techniques at work before learning them at school, and never stopped developing my skills in this area ever since.

Graphic Design

I worked at the university as an intern in the Communications and PR department for four years designing ads, graphics, invitations, taking photos, writing articles, etc. After that, I collaborated in a local magazine designing ads for clients.

UI Design

I had the opportunity to become involved with the User Interface world while working at SAMSUNG Electronics in Tijuana as part of the Intranet Team. I had to work closely with programmers to improve a variety of content that needed to be displayed in several screens throughout the company. I helped in the creation of the Intranet, transforming it from a plain information website to an actual interesting and attractive system.

UX Design

After having the experience of working with UI, I went back to general Graphic Design at DJO Global Manufacturer. I then realized that UI | UX was what interested me most and wanted to grow more in that field, so when I got the opportunity at Oracle I never hesitated about it, not for a second, even if I had to relocate. I decided that I could do both: desigining and programming; that's what also moved me to study the UX Advanced Certificate.
